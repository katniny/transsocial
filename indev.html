Hey! Welcome to TransSocial! Firstly, thank you a bunch for being here. I'm Katty, the founder of TransSocial and the lead developer.

As you noticed, we have a little notice that says that we're still in alpha. This is just because we're still working on a lot of TransSocial, and we're missing a lot of features or functionality that we're hoping to have. TransSocial is still stable and 100% usable!


What's planned to be added?

I don't really have a "pre-defined" list of things I want to add before going into different stages of development.

Basically this means, we go into a new stage of development when I think TransSocial is ready to.

For example, TransSocial got completely rewritten before going into alpha.


TransSocial's Plans for the Future

So what is TransSocial's (and my) plans for the future?

It's super simple. Updates, updates, updates. I plan on updating TransSocial A LOT over the course of 2024/2025. I hope to get through these stages of development:

  • InDev (Finished!)
  • Pre-Alpha (Finished!)
  • Alpha (Current)
  • Beta
  • Pre-Release
  • Release

(This list will be updated up until a little while after the release versions, when it will be removed).

(Please note that this list does NOT mean that all these versions will be released in 2024/2025, I just hope they are!)
